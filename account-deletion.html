Account Deletion Information

Globalink Market Place Ltd

Delete Your Account In‑App

You can delete your account directly from the app:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Delete Account

Follow the on‑screen confirmation and your request will be processed immediately.

Alternative: Request by Email

If you are having trouble deleting your account through the app, please send an email to globalinkmarketplaceltd@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: Your registered email address or phone number

Timeline

Once a deletion request is initiated, all personal data is permanently anonymized within 30 days.

Note: Certain transaction records and financial data may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations and anti‑fraud laws. After this period, the data is securely deleted.
